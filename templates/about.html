About Us

Nominator is a candidate shortlisting website which is made for the purpose of removing the burden of companies which they face while selecting the resumes of candidates who have applied for a job role. Through this website, they can easily shortlist resumes of candidates and take a personality test of the candidate which is saved as his/her profile. The organisation can check how eligible the candidate is for a particular job.
